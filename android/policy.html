Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application Learn English Playing Big Ben ("the App"). We respect your privacy. The App is designed to work offline and does not collect, store, or share personal data.

Data Collection and Sharing

The App does not collect personal or usage data and does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

Permissions Used

  • VIBRATE – used to provide haptic feedback in the App’s user interface.
  • INTERNET – declared in the manifest for technical compatibility. The App itself does not actively use this permission and does not send or receive user data over the internet.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases using Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled by Google Play. The App does not receive or store your payment information.

External Links

The App may allow you to open external resources (for example, your email client or the App’s Google Play page). When you open an external app or website, their privacy policies apply.

Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. Because the App does not collect personal data, no personal information is knowingly collected from any user.

Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit personal data, no user records are stored on our servers.

Changes to This Policy
